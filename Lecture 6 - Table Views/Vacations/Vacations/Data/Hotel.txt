The town of Velingrad is located in a most picturesque valley of the Chepinska river at a height of 750 meters above sea level. The mild and lovely climate is a perfect addition to the beautiful nature around. Winter is not hard, summer cool while autumn is warmer than spring. The sun is shining in this town for the better part of the year. Velingrad is rich in mineral springs suitable for treatment of different diseases such as ones of the nervous system, gynaecological diseases, diseases of the lungs and many others. With its variety of the types of mineral springs Velingrad occupies the first place in Bulgaria.
